SPRINTERO

Your sprint name generator

Sprintero add a command to your Slack, this command gives you possibility to randomly generate the name for your scrum sprint. Just use /sprintero command! And have fun :)

Privacy policy

Sprintero do not collect any user information - The only one is used is your team name to make nice redirect after app authorization. But it is never stored. Even for statistical purposes.
